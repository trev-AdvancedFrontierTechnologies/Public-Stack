Flying Triage Concept

Public-safe concept brief for emergency-response modeling, scene intelligence, and validation-first triage support research.

Posture: Concept-stage · Prototype-backed · Validation-first · Evidence-controlled

Entity: Advanced Frontier Technologies · UEI W2MEEUJACRJ3 · CAGE 1ZV02

Flying Triage concepts are not represented as certified medical devices, emergency-response systems, aviation systems, clinical decision systems, life-safety tools, or operationally approved products. Any such use would require formal validation, regulatory review, certification, and authorized deployment.

Purpose

Flying Triage is an AFT concept direction for combining scene understanding, simulation, digital-twin modeling, agentic coordination, and evidence-controlled decision support research for emergency-response and healthcare-adjacent environments.

Concept Areas

  • Scene-state modeling and situational awareness research.
  • Digital-twin concepts for patient flow, triage routing, and resource constraints.
  • Simulation-backed planning workflows.
  • Governed agentic support under strict human oversight.
  • Evidence packets for validation planning and partner review.

Validation Boundary

The current posture is concept-stage and prototype-backed. AFT's public materials are intended for technical discussion, partner discovery, and validation planning, not operational deployment.
