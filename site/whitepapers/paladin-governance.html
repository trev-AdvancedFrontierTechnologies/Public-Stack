Paladin Governance

Public-safe technical brief on governed agentic runtime oversight, evidence boundaries, and validation-first system control.

Posture: Prototype-backed · Validation-first · Evidence-controlled

Entity: Advanced Frontier Technologies · UEI W2MEEUJACRJ3 · CAGE 1ZV02

Purpose

Paladin Governance is AFT's oversight concept for managing state, priorities, evidence controls, validation boundaries, and safety posture across agentic and automated workflows.

Design Goals

  • Make agentic workflows auditable and explainable.
  • Preserve evidence boundaries and traceability.
  • Support controlled experimentation before deployment.
  • Separate prototype demonstrations from certified or operational use.
  • Provide governance checkpoints for partner diligence and validation planning.

Runtime Role

Paladin is intended to sit above agent, simulation, and evidence services as a state-machine oversight layer. It helps define what a system is allowed to claim, what evidence supports that claim, and what validation remains incomplete.

Company-Wide Relevance

Paladin Governance supports AFT's broader work in sovereign AI, simulation, public-safe software demos, ProofPacket-style diligence materials, emergency-response concepts, and healthcare-adjacent modeling boundaries.
