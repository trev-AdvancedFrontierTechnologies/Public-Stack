## Abstract

SMILEQT (Sovereign Multi-agent Integrated Lattice for Edge Quantum-compatible Technologies) is AFT’s prototype-backed runtime for governed multi-agent collaboration, simulation-linked reasoning, and evidence-controlled deployment.

It features a **21-agent discrete non-manifold lattice** with cortex-inspired functional mapping, Paladin governance, temporal-state modeling, and integration with the T.R.E.Y. OMEGA simulation layer.

All public claims are **prototype-backed**. This document does not assert production readiness, certified defense readiness, clinical readiness, or government endorsement.

## 1. Introduction and Problem Statement

Modern agentic systems often lack traceability, auditability, and strong governance boundaries. SMILEQT addresses this with a structured lattice architecture that prioritizes explainability, evidence linkage, and human-in-the-loop review before high-consequence actions.
